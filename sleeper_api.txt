### Rosters
# Get all rosters in a league
GET https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/<league_id>/rosters

### Matchups
# Get all matchups in a week
GET https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/<league_id>/matchups/<week>
# IDP2 N2 B.Martinez Br.FSB
curl  https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/705622575195168768/matchups/1 > matchups_705622575195168768_week1.json
# SF7 N4 R.White Br.FSB
curl  https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/709932171657297920/matchups/1 > matchups_709932171657297920_week1.json

### Playoff bracket
GET https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/<league_id>/winners_bracket
GET https://api.sleeper.app/v1/league/<league_id>/loses_bracket
